My Projects

Indie Music

I create my own music and listen to it whenever I need it. Sometimes I just can’t find the right song out there that I’d like to hear at times, so I compose it myself. Despite the usual bouts of criticism from others about the various elements I put in my music as well my inconsistency with genres, I rarely conform to what others say. I mainly make music for my personal interest and I could care less about any other motivations. Whatever piece I create is usually representative of some sort of sentiment I have in mind. I’m happy to share my music with everyone and I hope it somehow contributes to producing a positive effect on the world.
