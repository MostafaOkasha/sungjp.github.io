My Story

I got into programming during my sophomore year in high school through making online friends who had software development jobs (via a music-sharing site called plug.dj). They cultured me with Youtube videos ranging from documentaries on people like Aaron Swartz to making games with Python. From there, I became interested in Java but I was not able to take a computer science course at my high school. I self-studied the language the whole year until I was able to take the AP Computer Science exam without course credit. I then began writing bash scripts, of which my first was a notification sender that updated me on my friend's youtube activity every few hours. Following bash, I studied HTML/CSS and PHP/MySQL during my junior year in high school as I became the director and webmaster of my school's math competition site (www.rocketcitymath.org). Once I graduated high school, I took up an internship from www.setmine.com and worked on creating the dissolve/transition effect of their front page's image carousel. Through the internship, I was able to learn about Bootstrap, node.js, and how to collaborate using github repositories. I then began studying Python through Intro to Programming 101 during my first semester of college. I was able to work on cool projects that ranged from back-tracking sudoku solvers (https://github.com/sungjp/sudoku) to message encryption programs (https://github.com/sungjp/encryption). Following Python, I quickly took up Ruby as it was a similar language. Since then, I've been learning Ruby on Rails to make my own blog.

Max 7 Programming

I've been learning how to use Max 7 to create different programs that will take inputted MIDI data (from a keyboard or another electric instrument for example) and randomize notes in a particular key in various patterns and sequences to harmonize improvised playing sessions with my violin. As a pianist and violinist, I'm currently studying jazz theory, and I've been working on creating a virtual jazz partner to jam out with based on quantized raw audio input.
